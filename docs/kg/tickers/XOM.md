## 2026-05-03 → 2026-05-04 — Geopolitical hedge
- XOM added as wildcard hedge for Middle East supply shock — integrated oil provides geopolitical convexity.
- Brent sensitivity: XOM benefits from Brent spikes via upstream cash flows offset by downstream margin compression (higher feedstock), but net positive on >$80 Brent.

## Key Thesis
**Bull:** Highest-quality integrated oil balance sheet. Guyana barrels are among the lowest-breakeven in the world. Sustained oil risk premium from Hormuz/Iran adds upside skew. Strong capital return history.

**Bear:** Oil price mean-reversion on de-escalation is the primary risk. XOM's size means no single-asset leverage; upside is capped vs pure-play E&Ps. Downstream margins can compress if crude rises faster than product prices.
