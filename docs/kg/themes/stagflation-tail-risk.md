# Stagflation Tail Risk

Risk of simultaneous inflation acceleration (from energy/oil pass-through) and growth deceleration, creating the worst environment for risk assets. Characterized by: higher long-end yields, capped equity multiples, and preference for real assets/energy over duration.

## Macro Context (2026-04-10)
- VIX: 31 → 21.04 (easing but still elevated)
- Fed funds: 3.64%; 2Y: 3.79%; 10Y: 4.29%
- 10s-2s spread: ~0.50 (re-steepening, late-cycle)
- Regime: "transitioning" (not clean risk-on or risk-off)
- Oil bid from Hormuz disruption risk → energy inflation pass-through
