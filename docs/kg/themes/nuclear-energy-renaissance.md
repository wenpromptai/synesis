# Nuclear Energy Renaissance

Advanced nuclear technology companies targeting the growing power demand from AI datacenters and industrial electrification. Both SMR (small modular reactor) and fast fission approaches are being pursued. All current names are pre-revenue with milestone-driven valuations — binary risk on regulatory approvals and commercial deployment timelines.

## Key Characteristics (2026-04-12)
- Both companies are **pre-revenue** with **fortress balance sheets** (combined $2.5B cash, zero debt)
- Both carry **extreme valuation metrics** (Piotroski 1-2/9, negative ROE/ROA)
- Both have **critical insider selling clusters** (management distributing into strength)
- Regulatory timelines (NRC approvals, siting, construction) are the primary risk driver
- The "power + compute" narrative linking nuclear to AI datacenter demand is the bull case
- Bear case centers on FOAK (first-of-a-kind) deployment risk, cost overruns, and dilution
