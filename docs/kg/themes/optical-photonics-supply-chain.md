## 2026-04-10 — CPO Sector Update

Market has shifted from fundamental to growth/TAM-based valuation framework for entire optical stack. Key developments:

- **[[concepts/co-packaged-optics|CPO]]** entering initial deployment phase (2026), volume production 2027-2028. Market $603M (2026) → $2.9B (2032) at 29.7% CAGR.
- **NVIDIA** invested $2B each into [[tickers/LITE]] and [[tickers/COHR]] (March 2026) — signals optics = critical AI infrastructure
- **[[tickers/LITE]] joined S&P 500** (March 23, 2026) — forced index buying creating reflexive price dynamics. Stock up 990% in 1 year.
- **Sole-source risk**: Lumentum only supplier shipping 200G EMLs at volume. $400M+ [[concepts/optical-circuit-switch|OCS]] backlog for H2 2026.
- **TAM expanded**: Morgan Stanley estimates $90B AI optical market by 2028 (up from $30B in 2025). CPO penetration 4% → 30% by 2029.
- **[[concepts/indium-phosphide-substrates|InP]] supply chain**: China export controls on AXT/Tongmei substrates. [[tickers/AXTI]] crashed ~28% April 2 on tariff fears + insider selling + Herb Greenberg red flag.
- **Tariff overhang**: Section 232 deadline April 14 — binary risk for entire stack.
- **Adoption nuance**: CPO initially replaces copper, NOT pluggable fiber. Pluggable 800G/1.6T dominant for scale-out this decade. See [[sources/connections/cpo-adoption-curve-valuation]].
