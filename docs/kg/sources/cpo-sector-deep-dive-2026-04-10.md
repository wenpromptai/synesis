# CPO Sector Deep Dive — 2026-04-10

> [!info] Citation
> Zaikia Intelligence Desk. "CPO Sector Deep Dive." Web + X.com research via Claude Dispatch. April 10, 2026.

## Key Takeaways

1. **TAM explosion**: Morgan Stanley estimates AI optical communications market at $90B by 2028, up from ~$30B in 2025. CPO-specific market $603M (2026) → $2.9B (2032) at 29.7% CAGR.
2. **NVIDIA validation**: $2B each into LITE and COHR (March 2026) — signals optics = critical AI infrastructure, not just adjacent.
3. **Sole-source risk**: Lumentum is the only supplier shipping 200G-per-lane EMLs at volume. $400M+ OCS backlog for H2 2026. Double-digit EML price increases expected.
4. **InP chokepoint**: China export controls on InP substrates (Feb 2025) create geopolitical supply risk. AXT/Tongmei (~35% market share) requires per-order government permits (~60 business days).
5. **Valuation disconnect**: Market pricing 2028-2030 TAM penetration, but CPO volume production not until 2027-2028. Initial CPO replaces copper, not pluggable fiber. 12-18 month "faith gap."
6. **Section 232 catalyst**: April 14 tariff deadline creates near-term binary risk for entire optical stack.
7. **S&P 500 reflexivity**: LITE joining S&P 500 (March 23, 2026) forces passive buying → self-reinforcing price dynamics.
