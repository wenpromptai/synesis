May 1, 2026 was a classic risk-on tape: growth outperformed (QQQ +1.0%, XLK +1.5%) while volatility stayed contained and credit remained calm. The micro impulse was led by AAPL’s earnings beat and shareholder return actions (dividend increase + $100B buyback), reinforcing the mega-cap 'quality growth' bid. Geopolitics (Iran/Middle East tension + sanctions) remains the key tail-risk theme.
